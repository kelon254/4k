The white-bellied go-away bird has a call that sounds like'go-away'.It has grey plumage,a whitebelly,and a huge crest on top of its head.It also has white marks on its tail,which are visibke when the bird is in flight.

White -bellied go-away birds live in the arid savannahs and woodlands of kenya,Tanzania,Uganda,Sudan,Somalia,and Ethipia. The IUCN lists this birds as least concern ,with no major threats to its survival.
